📖 Industry Terms
LVR
Loan-to-Value Ratio — loan amount as % of property value. Big 4 max ~60% for commercial.
LMI
Lenders Mortgage Insurance — required when LVR exceeds lender threshold (usually >80% resi).
DSCR
Debt Service Coverage Ratio — net operating income / debt service. Must be >1.0, banks prefer >1.30.
ICR
Interest Coverage Ratio — NOI / interest expense.
WALE
Weighted Average Lease Expiry — average remaining lease term weighted by income.
NOI
Net Operating Income — gross rental income minus operating expenses.
ACR
Australian Credit Representative — registration under an aggregator's ACL.
RPL
Recognition of Prior Learning — fast-track Cert IV based on industry experience.
